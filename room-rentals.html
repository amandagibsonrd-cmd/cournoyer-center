Rental details

Important room rental information.

Clear expectations help each reservation run smoothly and protect the space for everyone who uses it.

Setup and teardown

Time is built into the rental.

Room rental comes with a 15-minute setup and tear down time. Bands are allowed to begin setup 15 minutes before the reservation and must be completely vacated 15 minutes after the reserved time.

Room tech

Support is present.

Each room reservation comes with a room tech present at all times.

Questions

Common room rental questions.

These answers help bands, musicians, and creators understand the spaces before reaching out.

How much is the performance space?

The performance space is listed at $35 per hour with backline and $25 per hour without backline, with a 2 hour minimum.

What is the sound booth for?

The sound booth is a smaller space suited for spoken word, voice-over, vocal recording, and simple audio or video projects.

Can students use the space?

Students enrolled in private instruction may be able to use the spaces for free based on availability and center policies.

Is backline included?

Backline equipment is available for the performance space. The rate depends on whether the rental includes backline use.

Are video or livestream services available?

Multi-camera and livestream support may be available by request. Contact the center for availability and package options.

How do I reserve a room?

Reach out with your desired date, time, room choice, number of people, project type, and equipment needs.

Need rehearsal or recording space?

Reach out with your date, time, room choice, project type, and equipment needs. The team can help confirm availability and next steps.
