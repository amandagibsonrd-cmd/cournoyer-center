About the center

A place where music becomes confidence.

Cournoyer Center for Music Performance exists to help students learn music in a way that feels encouraging, structured, creative, and connected to real performance experience.

What makes us different

Lessons, community, and performance under one roof.

Our purpose

Helping students grow as musicians and people.

The center is built around the belief that music lessons should do more than teach notes. They should build confidence, discipline, creativity, friendships, and a sense of belonging.

Skill

Strong musical foundations

Students build technique, rhythm, listening, practice habits, and instrument-specific skills.

Confidence

Encouraging instruction

Students are supported as they try new things, make mistakes, and see their progress over time.

Community

Music with others

Group programs and performance opportunities help students connect, collaborate, and grow together.

Our approach

Private lessons are the start. Performance is the difference.

Cournoyer Center gives students a path that can grow with them. A beginner may start with one-on-one lessons or a group class. As their confidence and skill develop, they may move into rehearsal settings, workshops, songwriting, or performance opportunities.

This approach helps students understand that music is not only something they practice alone. It is something they can share, collaborate on, and use to connect with others.
