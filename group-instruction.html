Questions

Common questions about group classes.

These short answers help parents understand how to choose a program and what to expect before registering.

Does my child need experience?

Many group programs are beginner-friendly. If a class requires experience, that can be noted in the program details.

How do I know which class is right?

Start with your child’s age, interest, and comfort level. You can also contact the center for help choosing the best fit.

Can group classes lead to private lessons?

Yes. Group instruction can be a great introduction before starting private lessons or joining a performance program.

Do classes change seasonally?

Yes. Workshops, camps, and group programs can rotate throughout the year based on interest, instructor availability, and seasonal scheduling.

Ready to find the right group program?

Reach out with your student’s age, interests, and experience level. The team can help guide you toward the best place to start.
