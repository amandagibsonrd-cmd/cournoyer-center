About Johnny Indovina

A songwriter with a long, storied career.

Johnny Indovina is known for decades of thoughtful songwriting, emotionally honest performances, and a devoted international following through his solo work and his band Human Drama.

Singer / Songwriter / Recording Artist

Johnny Indovina

Singer/songwriter Johnny Indovina has crafted a long, storied career through his thought-provoking work, both solo and with his band Human Drama. Through years of sold-out live performances and critically acclaimed albums, Indovina’s insightful songs about the frailties of the human condition have consistently found a receptive audience and a constantly renewed fan base.

Since 1985, he has fronted Human Drama. From their formation in New Orleans, through their rise within the mid-1980s Los Angeles club scene, their moving live shows, fueled by Indovina’s poetic imagery, built a huge and devoted following.

The combination of Indovina’s songwriting and the band’s growing popularity led to a contract with RCA Records in 1988, where they released Hopes Prayers Dreams Heart Soul Mind Love Life Death and Feel. In 1991, they moved to Triple X Records and produced what many consider Indovina’s masterpiece, The World Inside. A string of critical successes followed, including Pinups, The Human Drama EP, Songs of Betrayal, 14,384 Days Later, Solemn Sun Setting, In a Perfect World: The Best of Human Drama, Moments in Time, and Cause and Effect.

Despite flourishing primarily in the U.S. underground, Indovina and Human Drama achieved near-legendary status within music communities in Mexico and Latin America. Human Drama reunited for major shows in Mexico City, including their 30th anniversary performance in 2015, and later returned with new work including Broken Songs for Broken People, Blurred Images, and Ten Small Fractures.

In 2020, the documentary Seven Days in Mexico was released on Amazon Prime and Apple TV, following Johnny’s journey into rediscovering his musical purpose. In 2022, a book of his lyrics, My Bag of Secrets: The Words of Johnny Indovina, was published and made available on Amazon.

Johnny brings decades of writing, recording, touring, and creative resilience into each virtual songwriting session.

Questions

Common questions about the virtual program.

These answers help clarify that the songwriting sessions are online, personal, and centered around each writer’s creative goals.

Are the sessions virtual?

Yes. These are virtual one-on-one songwriting sessions, so participants can work with Johnny from anywhere.

How long is each session?

Each songwriting session is designed as a one-on-one, hour-long virtual lesson.

Do I need to already have songs written?

No. You can bring a finished song, an unfinished piece, a lyric idea, a melody, or a desire to learn how to begin.

Is this for beginners or experienced songwriters?

Both. The program is designed to support novice writers as well as experienced songwriters who want deeper feedback and creative direction.
